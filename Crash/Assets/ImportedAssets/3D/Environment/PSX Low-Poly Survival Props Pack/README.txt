PSX Low-Poly Survival Props Pack

Author: AQUICOR
Total Objects: 34
File Formats Included: .blend, .fbx, .obj, .glb
Textures: Included (see Textures folder)
Scale: 1 unit = 1 meter
Style: PSX / Low-Poly / Retro

CONTENTS
This pack includes a collection of low-poly survival equipment and emergency props created in a retro PSX art style. All models are lightweight, optimized and game-ready, ideal for survival, horror and post-apocalyptic environments.

Typical assets included:
- Melee weapons (knives, bats)
- Firearms and basic weapon props
- Flashlights and portable lights
- Walkie-talkies and communication devices
- Medical supplies and first aid items
- Survival and emergency equipment

INCLUDED FORMATS

Blender (.blend)
Contains all objects, materials and texture links.
Texture paths are relative. Open the .blend file from the root folder to load everything correctly.

FBX
Recommended for game engines.
Includes correct materials and transparency.
Note: FBX format may set Index of Refraction (IOR) to 1.5 by default.

GLB
Compact and engine-ready.
Ideal for Godot, WebGL and modern pipelines.

OBJ
Included for compatibility.
Note: OBJ format does not support alpha textures.
